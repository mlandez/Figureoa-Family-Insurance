Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our plans, eligibility, and how we work. Can't find your answer? Contact us directly.

Underwritten health insurance evaluates your individual health profile to determine your premium rate. Unlike ACA marketplace plans that charge the same rate regardless of health status, underwritten plans reward healthy individuals with lower premiums. During the application process, you'll complete a health assessment — no invasive exams required — and your premium is calculated based on your actual health risk rather than a statistical average.
ACA marketplace plans use community rating, which means everyone in the same age group and geographic area pays similar premiums regardless of health. Underwritten plans use individual health assessment to price your premium — so if you're healthy, you pay less. Additionally, our plans typically offer $0 copays, lower deductibles, and nationwide PPO network access that many marketplace plans don't provide.
Savings vary based on your age, health profile, location, and plan selection. Many of our clients see savings of 20% to 40% compared to equivalent ACA marketplace premiums. The best way to find out your specific savings is to schedule a free consultation — we'll provide a side-by-side comparison with no obligation.
Our underwritten plans are generally available to U.S. residents aged 25 to 64 who are in good health, not currently enrolled in Medicare or Medicaid, and looking for an alternative to ACA marketplace plans. Eligibility is ultimately determined during the underwriting process. If you're unsure whether you qualify, contact us — we can quickly assess your situation and let you know your options.
Pre-existing condition criteria vary by plan and carrier. Some conditions may be covered after a waiting period, while others may affect eligibility or premium pricing. We encourage you to schedule a free consultation so we can review your specific situation. Even if one plan doesn't work, we may have alternative options available to you.
On our qualifying plans, you pay $0 when you visit an in-network provider for covered services. No copay at the doctor's office, no copay at the specialist, and no copay for preventive care. Specific coverage details vary by plan tier, so we'll walk you through exactly what's covered during your consultation.
