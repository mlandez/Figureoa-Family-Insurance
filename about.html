Our Founder

Meet Marcos Figueroa.

After serving in the United States military, Marcos Figueroa transitioned into the health insurance industry with a clear mission: help everyday people access better healthcare coverage at fair prices.

During his service, Marcos learned that the most important things in life — health, family, security — should never be compromised by confusing paperwork or inflated premiums. When he discovered the world of underwritten health plans, he saw an opportunity to bring the same values he lived by in the military into a civilian career: honesty, thoroughness, and putting people first.

Figueroa Family Insurance was founded on the belief that healthy individuals shouldn't be penalized with the same rates as everyone else. Through medical underwriting, Marcos and his team connect clients with plans that reflect their actual health — not a statistical average.

"I served my country. Now I serve families. The mission never stops."
