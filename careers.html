Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need insurance experience to apply?

No prior insurance experience is required. We provide complete licensing support, study materials, and mentorship to help you get started from scratch.

Is this a W-2 or 1099 position?

This is an independent contractor (1099) role. Compensation is commission-based with no cap on earnings. Results vary based on individual effort and market conditions.

How do I get my health insurance license?

We provide study materials and exam preparation support to help you obtain your state health insurance license. Licensing requirements vary by state.

Can I work remotely?

Yes. This is a remote-friendly opportunity. You can work from anywhere and set your own schedule.

How long before I start earning?

Many advisors begin earning commissions within 30 days of obtaining their license. Your timeline depends on completing the licensing process and building your client base.

What states can I work in?

We operate nationwide across all 50 states. Your ability to advise clients depends on obtaining the appropriate state license.

Is there a base salary?

This is a commission-only role with no base salary. There is no cap on what you can earn. Your income directly reflects your effort and client relationships.

What kind of insurance will I sell?

You will advise clients on underwritten health insurance plans, including individual and family coverage with benefits like nationwide PPO access and competitive premiums.
