When you apply, we evaluate your health history through a simple assessment. No invasive exams or lengthy medical records required. Based on your health profile, we match you with the plan tier that offers the best combination of premiums and coverage.

Who qualifies

See if you're eligible.

Our underwritten plans are designed for generally healthy individuals. Here's a quick checklist to see if you may qualify.

Ages 25 to 64
U.S. resident in any of the 50 states
Generally good health with no major pre-existing conditions*
Not currently enrolled in Medicare or Medicaid
Looking for an alternative to ACA marketplace plans

*Eligibility is determined during the underwriting process. Pre-existing condition criteria vary by plan and carrier. Contact us to discuss your specific situation — we may still have options for you.

Underwritten health insurance evaluates your individual health profile to determine your premium. Unlike ACA marketplace plans that charge the same rate regardless of health status, underwritten plans can offer significantly lower premiums to individuals who are in good health.
