Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What is underwritten health insurance? +

Underwritten health insurance evaluates your individual health profile to determine your premium. Unlike ACA marketplace plans that charge the same rate regardless of health, underwritten plans reward healthy individuals with lower premiums and better coverage options.

How is this different from ACA marketplace plans? +

ACA plans use community rating — everyone pays the same. Our underwritten plans assess your health status, so if you're healthy, you can access significantly lower premiums, lower deductibles, and broader PPO networks.

How much can I save compared to marketplace plans? +

Qualifying healthy individuals can save up to 40% compared to equivalent ACA marketplace premiums. Actual savings depend on your health profile, age, location, and plan selection.

Who is eligible for these plans? +

These plans are ideal for self-employed individuals, freelancers, early retirees, and anyone not receiving employer-sponsored coverage who is in good health. We'll assess your eligibility during your free consultation.

Are pre-existing conditions covered? +

Coverage for pre-existing conditions varies by plan and carrier. During your consultation, we'll be transparent about what's covered and help you find the best option for your specific situation.

Is there really no cost for a consultation? +

Absolutely. Your initial consultation is completely free with no obligation. We'll review your health profile, compare plans, and give you clear options — no pressure.
